## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

- Understand the evolution of cybersecurity from reactive signature-based detection to proactive hypothesis-driven threat hunting
- Explain the Threat Hunting Loop and apply it conceptually to security scenarios
- Compare and contrast the Cyber Kill Chain and MITRE ATT&CK frameworks
- Articulate why endpoint telemetry is critical for threat detection
- Differentiate between structured, unstructured, and situational threat hunting
- Understand how threat intelligence integrates into the hunting process
- Analyze real-world case studies to recognize patterns in successful threat hunts
