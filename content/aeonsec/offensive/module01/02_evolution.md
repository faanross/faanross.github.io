The history of **penetration testing** (pentesting) mirrors the broader trajectory of computing itself - a journey from purely human-driven processes to increasingly automated and intelligent systems. Understanding this evolution is crucial for security professionals, as it reveals not only where the field has been, but where it's heading. Each phase built upon the limitations of its predecessor, introducing new capabilities while creating new challenges.

This chapter traces four distinct eras: the **Manual Era**, where human expertise was the sole driver; the **Automation Revolution**, where rule-based tools scaled human knowledge; the **Intelligence Layer**, where machine learning added adaptive capabilities; and the **Agentic Emergence**, where AI systems began making autonomous security decisions.
